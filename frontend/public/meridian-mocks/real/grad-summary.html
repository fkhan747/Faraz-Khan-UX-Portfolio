Why is graduate melt so high, and what can we do about it?

Of 2,308 admitted graduate applicants, 67.8% declined their offer. Graduate melt is structurally higher than undergraduate because applicants hold multiple funded offers and decide late.

The leverage is funding speed and clarity. Programs that release funding decisions earlier hold a materially higher share of admits. The international pool, 48% of applications and concentrated in India and China, also reacts to visa timelines, so earlier I-20 issuance moves yield.
